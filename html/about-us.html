About INSPRP

Feature-rich Resources

Our server comes with many resources, built in to provide a satisfying roleplay experience for everyone. As we say, the server was built with the player in mind.


Active Admins & Staff Support

Our admins and staff are able to meet your needs in as little time as possible. With an active Discord staff team and our very caring Mail Support Team, we will be there for your problems at all costs.

With a fully working CAD system in our server, it is very simple for First Responders to report information, while very enjoyable for the player, since it provides them with a unique and entertaining roleplaying experience.
