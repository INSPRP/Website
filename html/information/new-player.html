New to Server?

Getting you on track to being able to join a unique and entertaining experience, made for YOU.

Step 1: Prepare

Before you join our server, make sure you have done the following...

  • Joined our Discord
  • Take a look at our rules

After you have those and have a complete understanding of what we need from you, you are ready to get started.

Step 2: Learn

When you hop into the server, you may be confused as to what you are supposed to do? What are the commands, what are my options?

Civilian Commands

1. /me

Want to hand someone an ID? Want to pretend to do an action in-game? Want to do something we haven't implemented quite yet?

All you have to do is /me and what you want to act out.

Example: /me Gives TerroristMan43 Glock-19 with minipoints

2. /911

Have you been into a car wreck? Witnessed a robbery? Being chased down by the craziest killer in all of Los Santos? Well fear no more, as we have the /911 command

It's as simple as typing /911 and then your emergency right after!

Example: /911 I am being attacked near the fire station!

3. /tweet

Ever wanted to become the next Trump of Los Santos? Well, that's easy!

All you need to do is /tweet and then what you want to put up on your wall.

Example: /tweet I'm going to attack the PD here in about 5

4. /dv

Ever accidentally call in a Lamborghini and want it to be removed before Mr.BadAdmin68 walks by you?

All you need to do is get inside the car, and type /dv into the chat. It's as simple as that!

After taking in all of that information, you are now ready for Step 3.

P.S: Press F1 to open your game menu. Lets you spawn cars and RP properly. You're welcome :)

Step 3: Take Action

You now have what it takes to join the server. If you feel as though you are ready to join a one-and-only experience, meant with YOU in mind, go ahead and join our Discord!
