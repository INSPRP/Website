Community Rules

1. Duel Claning after Corporal or Civ Command Is prohibited. Or if You're Staff on another server

2. Coloring your name in steam to make it colorful in game is prohibited

3. Call-Sign must stay in steam name at all times

4. Police must be in RTO while in the server

5. Registered members must be at least 15 or older

6. Registered members must have 50+ hours on FiveM

7. ABSOLUTELY NO POACHING OR ATTEMPTED POACHING!

8. When you receive a strike you will automatically be demoted to the next lowest rank!

Verbal / Text Rules

1. Disrespect to anyone is prohibited

2. Racial slurs are strictly prohibited

3. Follow Chain of command

4. Homophobic Slurs are strictly prohibited

5. Discrimination based on Sex, Ethnicity, and Sexual Orientation is prohibited

Role-Playing Rules

1. No FailRP

2. No RDM / VDM

3. Power / Meta Gaming is prohibited

4. NLR (New Life Rule) Is expected to be followed

5. Cop Baiting Is Prohibited

6. Sexual / Erotic Role-Play is prohibited

7. Custom Cars Are Not To Be Abused

8. Explosives, Snipers, and Heavy weapons Are prohibited

9. Follow All Cool-down / Peacetime announcements

10. Monster Trucks, And Military vehicles are prohibited unless told otherwise

11. To fly aircrafts you must get admin permission

12. Animal RP is prohibited unless approved by an admin

13. Stealing Emergency Vehicles is prohibited unless you are both in the same RP (conditions apply)

14. No vehicle performance modifications of any kind as speedboost or torque. (this includes police vehicles). All vehicles have custom handling lines and are set to go as fast or as slow as permitted. Visual modifications are acceptable and encouraged.

15. No, No-Clipping unless Going to AOP, Setting up for an RP, or as Active Staff.
